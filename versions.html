Choose the version that fits your business

Every version of Lien Writer is built on the same core: the mechanics-lien forms your company needs to protect its lien rights. Which one is right for you comes down to two questions.

1

How many states?

Count the states where you need preliminary notices, releases, or mechanics liens. Single-State covers the one state where you do business, Tri-State covers up to three, and Multi-State covers all 50 states, Canada, and the US territories.

2

More than one company?

Only the Multi-State versions can create forms for an unlimited number of additional companies. If you file in the name of a DBA or a sister company, you may need Multi-State even in a single state.
